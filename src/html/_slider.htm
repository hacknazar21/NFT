What is NoHoomans Pub ?

A pub on an old rusty space station, where aliens serve you exotic beverages, and species from all over the galaxy spend their time drinking, chatting, playing games, and singing together.

The first module of a Sci-Fi MMORPG that utilizes the power of blockchain to support its player-driven economy.

NoHoomans Pub is a Social Hub that allows players to join and communicate with each other, play mini-games and get an early taste of the game that will follow.
