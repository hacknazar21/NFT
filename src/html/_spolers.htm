ROADMAP

The overall intensive growth of generative PFP collections in the NTF market creates an enormous level of competitiveness nowadays. It is very hard to stand out in the crowded space of collectibles, and deliver a special, unique product that will be able to allow potential owners to invest and expect high profitability. This is why our team works hard to implement a great quality into the project in order to generate a high ROI within the market.

To make this happen, we prepared a peculiar, eccentric PFP collection which will be considered an important aspect for progress in further strategic approach. Successful fundraising with our collection will provide a great value, which in turn, will insure the proper development, and outline the most important fields of the whole project itself.

The cost of the whole Project development is estimated for a 2 year period. This period of time implies the full preparation before releasing the finished game including all the added modules, level design, asset design, game mechanics and marketing to operate at the full potential.

Holders of Aliens are considered as early backers of the project. They will be rewarded with regular airdrops from upcoming collections and collaborations.

To provide the project with funds during the 2-year development period, we have prepared 3 main collections, which will also have special functionality in the game. They are:

  1. Alien NFT Collection - ___ size PFP collections of characters differ in traits and rarity. When “NoHoomans Pub” Pre-Alpha will be released, these characters will be remodeled and integrated for in-game use as playable skins and a key to access the NoHoomans Pub Location. Please keep in mind that the preparation of in-game skins will take time, most likely we will release them in bunches over 1 year.
  2. Quarters - a smaller collection of rooms used as personal property by players. There players will be able to store their inventory, socialize with other players, play mini-games, and more. The project is looking forward to providing rooms with a high level of rarity for active players. These active members will have an opportunity to design a model of their room directly with our team of developers by sharing ideas, considering personal requirements and creativity.
  3. Exotic Ships - this will be the smallest collection, and the most special one respectively. This collection will include unique spaceships that owners will use in the game when we introduce spaceship deathmatches in the game. Until then, spaceships will appear with the tagged names of proud owners in the “Hangar” section of the map.

The demo module of the Pub will be released and will operate as a “Social Hub” where all players will be able to use different characters within the playable area. It will be available only for PC users.

A primitive in-game multiplayer system will be implemented to allow gamers to interact with each other using integrated methods of communication and entertainment inside the demo version. This will serve as a base foundation for new game mechanics that will be added gradually.

The early demo will not require wallet connection or account registration. Both methods of the authorization will be added when spaceships, vendors, trading, and the whole token integrated ecosystem are introduced in the later version of the game. This will provide further access to the Pub location only for owners of our Alien PFP collection.

The Ship Module will be introduced to NoHoomans Pub in August of 2022, it will feature a complete interior design of the station, hangars for Small, Medium and Large ships and most importantly it will feature a 5 vs 5 battle mode, where players will be able to test out the ships and get a feel for how ship controls and UI will look like in Crypted Space.

This will be the first module of the game that will feature a massive UI update and a complete station level featuring zones like: The Square, Quarters, Black Market, and of course the Hangar. Ship modules and weapons will be added to the game as well as a Ship Vendor will be available on the station. Players will be able to freely fly around in the Sector(Warp Jumps won't be available yet).

We will introduce our token that will be used in-game as the currency that the players can use to buy and sell items. The supply will be around 25,000,000 where a part of the supply will be used for testing before the rest will be introduced in the game economy. After we are certain that there are no weak points - the remaining supply will be distributed to NPC’s, Factions and split among the players. This information is subject to change and more details on the token and the in-game economy will be released in our Whitepaper for Crypted Space.

By this point we are planning to have all the basic game mechanics ready for an Early Alpha, the players will be required to tie their wallets to the game client, all in-game items will be tied to NFT’s being displayed in the player's wallet, and the incorporation of the in-game token will slowly begin.

A small portion of the in-game world will be ready for players to explore. It will be filled with NPC’s, Quests, New Ships, Star-Systems, Planets, Stations, and various other in-game items will be added.

After NoHoomans Pub reaches this stage - it will mean that the first step towards building Crypted Space will be complete. We will successfully demonstrate to backers and investors our capabilities and the potential for the project going forward.
