ROADMAP

Aliens, Quarters and Exotic Ships are the three collections that we are planning to release. Their main goal is to help fund the development of the game and attract more investors to the project. Right now, we are getting ready to release our first collection:

Alien PFP Collection - a collection of characters that differ in traits and rarity. When “NoHoomans Pub” Pre-Alpha will be released, these characters will be integrated for in-game use as playable skins and a key to access the NoHoomans Pub module of the Game. Please keep in mind that the preparation of in-game skins will take time, most likely we will release them in small bunches over 1 year.

The demo module of the Pub will be released and will operate as a “Social Hub” where all players will be able to use different characters within the playable area. It will be available only for PC users.

A primitive in-game multiplayer system will be implemented to allow gamers to interact with each other using integrated methods of communication and entertainment inside the demo version. This will serve as a base foundation for new game mechanics that will be added gradually.

The early demo will not require wallet connection or account registration. Both methods of the authorization will be added when spaceships, vendors, trading, and the whole token integrated ecosystem are introduced in the later version of the game. This will provide further access to the Pub location only for owners of our Alien PFP collection.

Our Ship Module will be introduced to NoHoomans Pub in August of 2022, it will feature a complete interior design of the station, hangars for small, medium, and large ships. Most importantly it will feature a "5 vs. 5" battle mode, where players will be able to test out their ships and learn about the controls and UI functioning in Crypted Space.

This will be the next module of the game that will feature a massive UI update and a complete station level that contains areas like Town Square, living quarters, black market, hangar, and more.

Ship modules and weapons will be added to the game as well as a Ship Vendor that will be available on the station. Players will be able to freely fly around in the Sector(Warp Jumps won't be available yet).

Cost of the whole Project development is estimated for 2 years. This period implies the full preparation before releasing the finished game including all the added modules, level design, asset design, game mechanics, and marketing to operate at its full potential.

According to our plans, November 2022 is the month when NoHoomans will reach a stage suitable for a Pre-Alpha. This is when the team will start focusing heavily on the development of the main game.

Introduction of our token that will be used in-game as a currency that the players can use to buy and sell items. The supply is yet to be determined but a part of it will be used for testing before the rest will be introduced in the game economy.

After we are certain that there are no weak points - the remaining supply will be distributed to NPC’s, Factions and split among the players. This information is subject to change and more details on the token and the in-game economy will be released in our Whitepaper for Crypted Space.

By this point, we are planning to have all the basic game mechanics ready for the Alpha state. This version of the game will require players to tie their wallets to the game website. All in-game items will be tied to NFT’s being displayed in the player's wallet, and the integration of the in-game token will begin to operate.

A small portion of the in-game world will be ready for players to explore. It will be filled with NPC’s, Quests, New Ships, Star-Systems, Planets, Stations, and various other in-game items will be added.

After NoHoomans Pub reaches this stage - it will mean that the first step towards building Crypted Space will be complete. Our capabilities and the full potential of the project’s further successful development will be constantly demonstrated to the holders and investors
