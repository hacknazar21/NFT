What is NoHoomans Pub ?

The Pub on an old rusty space station - first special module of the upcoming game that will provide limited access exclusively for Holders of our NFT collection.

The Pub will serve as a Social Hub, where players interact with each other via voice/text chat, spend time watching movies, singing karaoke, and playing different mini-games such as pool, poker, blackjack, darts, arcades, and more.
